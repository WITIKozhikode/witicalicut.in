Welcome to Govt. ITI For Women Kozhikode

"An institution with a difference" - We ensure the steady flow of skilled female workers, enhance industrial production, and provide employable training to cultivate a technical and industrial attitude for self-employment.

Govt. Industrial Training Institute for Women, Kozhikode, is committed to providing the best academic and competitive training through specially designed programs, faculty, and support staff. We set the path in Women empowerment through Vocational Training.

Today, our campus is home to 568 trainees, supported by 27 dedicated faculty members and 26 staff members. We take pride in producing more than 472 skilled female professionals annually, contributing significantly to workforce development and women's empowerment.

How to Reach: 10 Kilometers from Kozhikode Railway station, 30 Kilometers from Calicut Airport, 9 Kilometers from KSRTC Bus stand Kozhikode.
