Welcome to Govt. ITI For Women Kozhikode

"An institution with a difference"............. We ensure the steady flow of skilled female workers, enhance industrial production, and provide employable training to culture a technical and industrial attitude for self-employment.

Govt. Industrial Training Institute for Women, Kozhikode, is committed to providing the best academic and competitive training through specially designed programs, faculty, and support staff. We set the path in Women empowerment through Vocational Training.

The campus today is home to **568 trainees**, **27 faculties**, and **26 staff** supporting them. It brings out more than **472 young female professionals annually**.

**How to Reach:** 10 Kilometers from Kozhikode Railway station, 30 Kilometers from Calicut Airport, 9 Kilometers from KSRTC Bus stand Kozhikode.
